Building Sint Maarten

At Hives, we specialize in high-end architectural construction for the commercial and residential sectors on Sint Maarten.

We also pride ourselves on providing skilled manpower and quality tradespeople to meet the demands of the ever-growing construction industry here on the island.

Manpower

Manpower

Hives is the preferred supplier of workers to the construction industry on Sint Maarten.

Our goal is to meet the increasing demands for manpower and quality tradespeople to the construction industry here on the island. We have a proven and tested process of employment selection and all our teams come fully insured, with full PPE and ready for work.

Our site supervisors can work alongside Prime Contractor management if necessary, and full on-site training can be offered depending upon the needs of our clients. Mobilization is typically within one week upon contract agreement.

About us

Hives is a construction management company based in Sint Maarten and specializing in high-end architectural projects.

We focus on building and renovating hotels and private residences, which demands smart thinking and excellent craftsmanship. Whether it’s new construction from the ground up, or a custom renovation, our teams are committed to realizing your vision – right down to the finest detail.

Strong project management is the foundation of our business. Extremely thorough budgets, consistent budget tracking, detailed schedules and transparent invoicing are some of the key elements that ensure our projects run on time and on budget.

We look forward to meeting you in person to explain our construction process and answer any questions you may have.
