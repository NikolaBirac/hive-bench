Residental Construction

Hives specializes in working with homeowners to build or restore beautiful living spaces.

Our clients want the process to run smoothly, stay on budget and maintain accurate completion schedules throughout. Transparent invoicing and weekly budget reports are our standard processes. Product knowledge and high level craftsmanship create the perfect spaces for our clients which makes Hives a perfect design build choice. We can offer full design and rendering services as part of the process.

Commercial Construction

Restoring after a national emergency or working with Construction Developers to renovate or build new, Hives understands the importance of flexibility and accountability.

Experience of working to tight time-lines and budgets, we can also offer advice on Contracts, Management strategies and product supply, making Hives an excellent building partner in your project.

Manpower

Hives is the preferred supplier of workers to the construction Industry.

Our goal is to meet the increasing demands for Manpower and quality Tradesmen to the construction industry within the West Indies. Hives has a proven and tested process of employment selection and all our teams come fully insured, full PPE and ready for work.

Our team Superintendents can work alongside Prime Contractor management if necessary and full on-site training can be offered depending upon the needs of our clients. Mobilization is typically within one week upon contract agreement.

About

Hives is a construction management company specializing in high-end architectural projects.

Our focus is on building and renovating homes and hotels that demand smart thinking and excellent craftsmanship. Whether it’s a detailed home from the ground up or a custom renovation, our crew is committed to your vision, right down to the finest detail.

Strong project management is the foundation of our business. Extremely thorough budgets, consistent budget tracking, detailed schedules and transparent invoicing are some of the key elements that ensure our projects run on time and on budget.

We look forward to meeting you in person to explain our construction process and answer any questions you may have.
